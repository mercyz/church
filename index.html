Welcome To MCM

Mike Cassidy Ministries has a God-ordained mandate to present the Gospel of Jesus Christ to the lost, disciple the nations and provide humanitarian aid.

We are called to reach every city and transform communities through the ministry of the love of Jesus with practical teaching of the word of Faith. With the prayer and support of our friends and partners, it is our goal to reach every nation, every city, one life at a time, with the Gospel.
